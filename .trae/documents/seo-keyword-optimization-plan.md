# SEO关键字优化计划：不锈钢保温壶制造商网站

## 一、当前网站关键字分析

### 现有Title
```
Jiangmen BaoAo Hardware Appliances | Premium Vacuum Flask&Airpot Manufacturer
```

### 问题诊断
1. **品牌名优先** - 公司名"Jiangmen BaoAo"放在最前面，但买家搜索时不会搜这个
2. **缺少核心产品词** - "Vacuum Flask"位置靠后
3. **缺少关键修饰词** - 无"stainless steel"、"China manufacturer"、"OEM/ODM"等高价值B2B词
4. **缺少购买意图词** - 无"wholesale"、"supplier"、"factory"等商业词

## 三、优化方案

### 3.1 首页Title优化（index.html）

**当前：**
```html
Jiangmen BaoAo Hardware Appliances | Premium Vacuum Flask&Airpot Manufacturer
```

**建议改为（英文版）：**
```html
Stainless Steel Vacuum Flask & Airpot Manufacturer | OEM/ODM Factory China - BaoAo
```

**建议改为（葡萄牙语版）：**
```html
Fabricante de Garrafa Térmica Aço Inoxidável | OEM/ODM China - BaoAo
```

**建议改为（西班牙语版）：**
```html
Fabricante de Termos Acero Inoxidable | OEM/ODM China - BaoAo
```

### 3.2 Meta Description优化

**建议（英文）：**
```html
<meta name="description" content="China professional vacuum flask & airpot manufacturer with 14+ years experience. OEM/ODM services, 304/316 stainless steel, LFGB/FDA certified. Annual capacity 1M+ pieces. Low MOQ, fast delivery.">
```

**建议（葡萄牙语）：**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Fabricante profissional de garrafa térmica a vácuo e airpot da China. 14+ anos de experiência. OEM/ODM, aço inoxidável 304/316, certificado LFGB/FDA. Capacidade anual 1M+ peças.">
```

**建议（西班牙语）：**
```html
<meta name="description" content="Fabricante profesional de termos y airpot de vacío de China. 14+ años de experiencia. OEM/ODM, acero inoxidable 304/316, certificado LFGB/FDA. Capacidad anual 1M+ piezas.">
```

### 3.3 页面内容关键字布局

#### H1标题优化
```html
<!-- 当前 -->
<h1>Premium Vacuum Flask&Airpot Manufacturer<br>from China</h1>

<!-- 建议 -->
<h1>Stainless Steel Vacuum Flask & Airpot<br>OEM Manufacturer from China</h1>
```

#### H2小标题优化
- ✅ About Us → **China Professional Vacuum Flask Factory**
- ✅ Our Products → **Premium Insulated Flasks & Airpots**
- ✅ Production Process → **Professional Manufacturing Process**
- ✅ Quality Assurance → **LFGB/FDA Certified Quality**
- ✅ Custom Designs → **OEM/ODM Custom Design Service**
- ✅ Contact → **Get a Quote from China Factory**

#### 页面正文关键字密度
建议在以下位置自然融入关键字：
1. Hero描述区 - 加入"stainless steel"、"OEM factory"、"wholesale"
2. About区 - 加入"manufacturer in China"、"14 years experience"
3. 产品质量区 - 加入"304/316 stainless steel"、"LFGB certified"
4. 定制服务区 - 加入"custom colors"、"logo printing"、"low MOQ"

### 3.4 Alt标签优化（图片SEO）

```html
<!-- 产品图片 -->
<img src="..." alt="1.0L Stainless Steel Vacuum Flask Airpot Model 66">

<!-- 工厂图片 -->
<img src="..." alt="China Vacuum Flask Factory Production Workshop">

<!-- 工艺图片 -->
```
